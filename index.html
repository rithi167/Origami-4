Pigeon Origami Instructions

Fold the paper in half and then unfold it. Fold the paper in half the other way. Fold both layers of paper to the right. Fold the top layer of paper to the left. Then, Fold the model in half down. Fold the top flap of paper up and then repeat on the other side. Fold the paper then unfold it. Make an Inside Reverse Fold using the crease from the previous step.


Teddy Origami Instructions

Fold and unfold the paper in half both ways. Fold both sides in to the centre. Fold the right side of the paper over along the dotted line and then fold it back out like on the other side. Make a small fold on each side. Open up the paper along the dotted lines. Lift it up and push it flat. Next, Fold the top of the top layer of paper. Fold both sides in along the dotted lines. Fold the top of the paper down along the dotted line. Fold the paper underneath along both the dotted lines. Make two Pleat Folds to form the ears. Fold the paper in half a little bit to make the model a bit 3D.
